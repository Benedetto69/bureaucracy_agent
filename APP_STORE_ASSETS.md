## Description (English)

Bureaucracy helps you understand if it makes sense to contest a traffic fine.

WHAT THE APP DOES:
- Analyzes the ticket text via OCR
- Identifies possible formal, procedural, or substantive defects
- Estimates the probability of appeal success
- Generates a draft response to customize
- Calculates the economic convenience of the appeal

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Photograph or upload the ticket
2. The app extracts the text automatically
3. Receive an analysis with the critical points detected
4. You decide whether to proceed with the appeal

IMPORTANT - DISCLAIMER:
This app does NOT provide legal advice and does NOT replace a lawyer. The analyses are indicative and based on artificial intelligence algorithms. Success probabilities are estimates, not guarantees. For important decisions or complex cases, always consult a legal professional.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY:
- Your data is processed and deleted within 24 hours
- Images stay on your device
- No mandatory account
- Encrypted local storage
- You can delete all data at any time

## Review Notes for Apple (Optional)

This app helps users analyze traffic tickets to understand if they might have grounds for appeal.

IMPORTANT CLARIFICATIONS:
1. The app does NOT provide legal advice
2. All analyses are clearly marked as "indicative"
3. Users must confirm they understand limitations before generating documents
4. Success probabilities are clearly labeled as estimates
5. Users are encouraged to consult lawyers for complex cases

The app processes ticket text via OCR on-device (iOS Vision framework) and sends only the extracted text to our analysis API. Images are never uploaded. All data is deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

Demo credentials: Not required - the app works with real user-uploaded documents.
